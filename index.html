A LITTLE ABOUT ME

As someone who enjoys both the frontend & backend, I can be your next Full Stack Software Developer. Diving deep into code to create logic, solve algorithms or debug code are a few things I enjoy doing. As much as I enjoy immersing myself in code, I am always seeking an opportunity to collaborate with others on project and ideas. As I continue to discover and learn new languages and frameworks, I am constantly looking for new challenges in the software developer industry.

Other than writing code, I also have a passion for youth development. Before I became a software developer, I was working with youth in various settings. I have worked with youth in the classroom, coaching in various sports and developing curriculum for summer camps. I enjoy learning about different ways we can help engage the youth in community. With my experience in finding creative ways to find activities involving youth, it has helped me think outside the box when working with code..
